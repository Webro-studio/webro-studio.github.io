Frequently Asked Questions

What types of websites do you specialize in for the Bangladesh market?

We build everything from personal portfolios and blogs to comprehensive business sites, booking systems, and robust e-commerce platforms tailored for businesses in Bangladesh. We focus on local payment gateway integrations (like bKash, Nagad) and solutions that resonate with the local audience.

How long is the typical website development process?

A simple one-page site might take 1-2 weeks. A more complex business or e-commerce site could take 4-8 weeks or more. The timeline depends on the project's complexity, features, content availability, and client feedback speed.

What's your process for building a website?

Our process is client-focused: 1. Discovery Call (understand your needs & goals in Bangladesh), 2. Project Scoping & Quotation, 3. Design & Development (with regular feedback loops), 4. Review & Revisions, 5. Final Payment & Launch, 6. Post-Launch Support & Training.

Do you provide website maintenance and support after launch?

Yes, we offer various website maintenance packages to ensure your site remains updated, secure, and performs optimally. We can discuss ongoing support options tailored to your needs.
